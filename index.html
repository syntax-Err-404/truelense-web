HOW IT WORKS

Six detection layers.
One clear verdict.

🔬

Noise analysis

Every camera introduces a unique grain signature. AI generators produce patterns that fall outside natural camera output ranges.

📐

Edge structure

Real images have naturally varied edge sharpness. AI tools produce edges that are too clean and too consistent to be real.

🎨

Color distribution

The color entropy of AI-generated content falls outside the range of what real cameras capture in natural conditions.

💡

Lighting consistency

Real scenes have physically consistent light sources. AI models produce subtle inconsistencies in shadow and highlight behavior.

🎬

Temporal motion

For video, we analyze how motion evolves between frames. AI-generated video produces motion patterns real cameras cannot replicate.

🗂

Metadata integrity

Real photos embed technical capture data. AI-generated images contain no camera metadata — a high-confidence signal on its own.
